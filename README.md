## About

I am a Registered Nurse with 21 years of high-acuity clinical experience (ER, PACU, Surgical Services) transitioning into Cybersecurity and Information Assurance. My background is rare in this field: I understand healthcare infrastructure — Epic EHR build, clinical workflow design, regulatory compliance — from the frontline up.

I spent years as an Informatics Analyst III and Epic Instructional Designer bridging the gap between complex technical systems and the clinicians who depend on them. That experience gave me a practitioner's understanding of where healthcare data lives, how it moves, and what happens operationally when it's compromised.

I am currently completing my **Master of Science in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance (MSCSIA)** at Western Governors University, with a focus on **Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)**. I am pursuing the **CGRC** and **CRISC** certification roadmap, aligned with my goal of proactively identifying and mitigating risk rather than reacting to incidents after the fact.

## What Makes This Portfolio Different

Most cybersecurity candidates understand the frameworks. Few understand what a nurse sees at 2 AM when the EHR goes down and she cannot pull a patient's medication history.

Healthcare is the most targeted sector in cybersecurity. Ransomware attacks on hospital systems are not data breaches — they are patient safety events. My 21 years in clinical environments give me a ground-level understanding of operational risk that cannot be taught in a classroom. I bring that perspective to every risk assessment, policy, and compliance framework I work with.
